Sapnon Ki Rani - Part 1

Arjun har raat ek hi sapna dekhta tha. Ek khoobsurat ladki, jiske baal hawa mein udte the aur aankhen sitaaron jaisi chamakti thi.

"Tum kaun ho?" woh sapne mein poochta.

Lekin woh ladki sirf muskurati aur gayab ho jaati.

Ek din Arjun college mein naye students ke beech thi... wahi ladki!

"Yeh... yeh toh sapnon wali hai!" uska dil zor se dhadakne laga.

Ladki ka naam Meera tha. Woh naye sheher mein aayi thi.

"Hi, main Meera," usne introduce kiya.

Arjun kuch bol nahi paaya. Kya yeh sach tha ya phir ek aur sapna?

"Kya hua? Tum theek ho?" Meera ne poocha.

"Haan... haan, main Arjun hoon," usne somehow jawab diya.

Usi din se unki dosti shuru hui. Lekin Arjun ke mann mein sawaal tha - kya Meera ko pata hai ki woh uske sapnon mein aati hai?

Raat ko phir wahi sapna aaya. Meera wahan thi.

"Ab toh mil gaye na hum," sapne mein Meera ne kaha.

Arjun jab utha, uska dil aur bhi confused tha.
