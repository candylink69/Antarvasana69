Chandni Raat Ka Raaz - Part 5 (Final)

Din guzarte gaye. Priya ne apne ghar mein Rahul ke baare mein batane ka faisla kiya.

"Mummy, Papa... mujhe kuch kehna hai," usne ek shaam ko kaha.

Uske maa-baap ne uski baat suni. Priya ne himmat se sab kuch bata diya - Rahul ke baare mein, unke pyaar ke baare mein.

Pehle toh silence tha. Phir uski mummy ne kaha, "Beta, pyaar galat nahi hai. Lekin hume us ladke se milna hoga."

Priya ki aankhon mein aansu aa gaye - khushi ke aansu.

Agle hafte Rahul Priya ke ghar aaya. Nervous tha, lekin confident bhi.

"Uncle, Aunty... main aapki beti se bahut pyaar karta hoon. Main usse khush rakhunga, yeh mera waada hai."

Uske maa-baap ne Rahul ko dekha. Uski seedhi baatein, uski sacchi aankhein... sab kuch sahi tha.

"Theek hai... hum maan gaye," Priya ke papa ne kaha.

Us raat garden mein Priya aur Rahul phir mile. Lekin aaj chup kar nahi, balki khul kar.

"Humari kahani ab sach mein shuru hogi," Rahul ne kaha.

Chandni phir chamak rahi thi. Aur is baar woh ek naye adhyay ki gawah thi.

--- THE END ---
