Chandni Raat Ka Raaz - Part 3

Unke hoth ek doosre ke karib aaye. Pehli baar Priya ne itni nazdeeki mehsoos ki thi kisi ke saath.

"Kya yeh sahi hai?" usne apne aap se poocha.

Lekin jawab dene ka waqt nahi tha. Rahul ke hothon ne uske hothon ko chhua aur ek alag duniya khul gayi.

Woh kiss dheemi thi, pyaar bhari thi. Jaise do dil ek ho rahe hon.

Jab woh alag hue, dono ke chehron par ek alag si chamak thi.

"Yeh meri zindagi ka sabse khoobsurat pal hai," Rahul ne kaha.

Priya ne sirf muskurahat di. Uske paas shabd nahi the.

Woh dono bench par baith gaye, ek doosre ke haath thaame. Raat dheere dheere guzar rahi thi lekin unhe uski parwaah nahi thi.

"Tumne kabhi socha tha ki ek anjaan se pyaar ho jayega?" Priya ne poocha.

"Nahi... lekin tumhe dekh kar samajh aaya ki pyaar planning se nahi hota. Yeh toh bas ho jaata hai."

Chandni ab aur bhi chamak rahi thi. Jaise woh bhi unki kahani ka gawah ban rahi ho.

"Kal phir milenge?" Rahul ne umeed se poocha.

"Zaroor... isi waqt, isi jagah."
