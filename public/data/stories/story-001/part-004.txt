Chandni Raat Ka Raaz - Part 4

Agle din se ek naya silsila shuru ho gaya. Har raat Priya aur Rahul garden mein milte.

Unki baatein, unki hasi, unka pyaar... sab kuch chandni raaton mein khilta gaya.

"Tum jaanti ho, main tumhare bina ab ek din bhi nahi reh sakta," Rahul ne ek raat kaha.

Priya ne uski aankhon mein dekha. "Main bhi Rahul... tum meri zindagi ka hissa ban gaye ho."

Woh raatein unke liye khaas thi. Duniya so jaati thi aur woh jaag kar ek doosre ke saath waqt guzaarte.

Lekin pyaar mein mushkilein bhi aati hain.

Ek din Priya ke papa ne kaha, "Beta, humne tumhare liye rishta dhundh liya hai. Ladka bahut acha hai."

Priya ka dil doob gaya. Usne Rahul ke baare mein socha.

"Papa... mujhe thoda waqt chahiye," usne kaha.

Usi raat usne Rahul ko sab bataya. Rahul ka chehra utar gaya.

"Toh ab kya hoga?" usne poocha.

"Pata nahi Rahul... lekin main tumhe kho nahi sakti."

Chandni wahi thi, lekin aaj raat mein udaasi thi.
