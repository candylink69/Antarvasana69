Chandni Raat Ka Raaz - Part 1

Raat ka andheraa chaaon ki tarah faila tha jab Priya apni balcony mein khadi thi. Hawa mein thandi si khushbu thi aur chandni ki roshni uske chehre par chamak rahi thi.

"Kitni khoobsurat raat hai," usne khud se kaha.

Tabhi uski nazar samne wali khidki par padi. Rahul wahan khada tha, uski taraf dekhta hua. Dono ki nazrein mili aur ek ajeeb si khamoshi chha gayi.

Priya ne muskurate hue haath hilaya. Rahul ne bhi jawab diya. Yeh silsila kai dinon se chal raha tha, lekin aaj kuch alag tha.

"Kya tum neeche aa sakti ho?" Rahul ne ishaare se poocha.

Priya ka dil zor se dhadakne laga. Kya usay jaana chahiye? Ghar mein sab so chuke the. Raat ka sannata aur chandni ki chamak... sab kuch ek kahani ki tarah lag raha tha.

Usne ek baar apne kamre ki taraf dekha, phir neeche jaane ka faisla kar liya.

Garden mein gulab ki khushbu thi. Rahul wahan bench par baitha tha, uska intezaar karte hue.

"Tum aa gayi," usne khushi se kaha.

"Haan... pata nahi kyun, lekin aana zaroori laga," Priya ne jawab diya.

Dono kuch der chup rahe. Phir Rahul ne Priya ka haath pakda. Uski ungliyan garmi se bhari thi.

"Main bahut dinon se tumse kuch kehna chahta tha..."
